  • IEEE WORKBENCH

    August 2019

    During my junior year I became heavily involved with UK IEEE (Institute of Electrical and Electronic Engineers), especially the robotics team. The big problem throughout the year is we had no easy way to transport and store our tools and materials. So I designed a custom workbench that would fit our needs and be very flexible for any future needs. It has 3 drawers, a lot of pegboard space, a vice, castors for mobility, and it's modular! It comes apart so we can take it to robotics competitions if we want to. Then I spent most of August 2019 building the actual thing. I still need to do a few small things to it, but it is now at UK and being used!
